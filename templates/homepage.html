Abstract

This report details the gain calibration of four photo-multiplier tubes (PMT) and their chain of electronics. The chain of electronics used to calibrate the PMT's was also calibrated. Each PMT has its own Ortec 113 pre-amplifier (PM) and each PM was calibrated using the same amplifier and oscilloscope for consistency. A total of 4 PM's and 4 PMT's were calibrated, which were labeled PM0-3 and PMT0-3, respectively. PM0 had a measured gain of 13,650 $\pm$ 75.52, PM1 had a measured gain of 13,905 $\pm$ 73.46, PM2 had a measured gain of 14,067 $\pm$ 44.74, and PM3 had a measured gain of 13,781 $\pm$ 64.12. PMT0 had a measured gain of 226,139 $\pm$ 44,773.53, PMT1 had a measured gain of 324,437 $\pm$ 22,213.29, PMT2 had a measured gain of 4892,00 $\pm$ 47,107.11, and PMT3 had a measured gain of 498,517 $\pm$ 106,086.12. Details on how this calibration was performed and data analyzed are within this report.
